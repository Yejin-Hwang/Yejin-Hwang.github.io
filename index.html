About Me

Hi, I'm Yejin 👋

I'm finishing my MS in Data Science at Texas A&M University–Corpus Christi (GPA 4.0, Graduate Fellow), graduating May 2026. My thesis integrates Reddit-derived FinBERT sentiment with Temporal Fusion Transformer models across 10 large-cap U.S. equities — achieving a Sharpe ratio of 2.74 and 20.19% cumulative return.

Before data science, I worked as a clinical data researcher at Samsung Medical Center in Seoul, supporting epidemiological studies under Johns Hopkins-affiliated Principal Investigators. I hold dual B.S. degrees from Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), where I won the President Award for R&D Innovation.

I thrive at the intersection of rigorous statistical methodology and practical ML engineering — building systems that are both theoretically sound and production-ready.
